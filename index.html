Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Thank you for using Sociogenix. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Our app allows users to upload images for analysis to generate captions and hashtags.

We may temporarily process:

  • Images uploaded by the user
  • Basic technical data (such as app performance and usage data)

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, phone number, or address.

2. How We Use Information

The information is used strictly for:

  • Generating captions and hashtags
  • Improving app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store your images permanently. Images are processed temporarily and are automatically deleted after processing.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party AI services to analyze images and generate captions/hashtags. These services may process your data only for providing results and do not store your data permanently.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except when required for processing through trusted services or when required by law.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Notifications

Our app may request permission to send push notifications in order to provide users with important updates, announcements, reminders, and app-related alerts.

  • Notifications are sent only after the user grants notification permission.
  • Users can disable notifications anytime from their device settings.
  • We do not use notifications to collect personal data directly.
  • Third-party notification providers (such as Firebase Cloud Messaging) may process device tokens required to deliver notifications.

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver push notifications. FCM may collect device identifiers/token necessary for notification delivery.
